The Dataset

Iran Caravanserai Dataset consist of Geographical Location database of 160 Iranian Caravanserai with their aerial images.

Dataset features

  • Geo-Locations dataset: Geo-Locations of 160 Iranian Caravanserai [interactive map in this link].
  • Classification dataset: Consist of 319 (600x600 px) RGB aerial images of Caravanserai and 749 (600x600 px) RGB images of Negative/Other places from Google Map and Bing Map.
  • Object detection dataset:1077 YOLO annotations and (600x600 px) RGB aerial images of Caravanserai and Negative/Other places.
